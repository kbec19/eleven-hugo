About

A strategist built at the intersection of fields.

Kristina Becvar brings a rare combination to the organizations she advises: deep operational and administrative expertise earned across 17 years of executive leadership, genuine policy and civic reform credentials from serving at the center of the national pro-democracy field, and rigorous data analytics training at the graduate level.

As Executive Director of the Bridge Alliance, Kristina led the national coalition at the heart of the civic renewal ecosystem — managing external relationships, setting organizational strategy, and activating a network spanning hundreds of partner organizations, funders, and advocates. She departed when the organization completed its transition to a new model, and brings that full-field perspective to her consulting work.

Her analytical toolkit (R, Python, SQL, Tableau) and published academic research complement — rather than replace — hard-won instincts about what actually moves organizations and coalitions forward.

Services

What Eleven Insight offers.

Engagements are tailored, not templated. The work typically falls across three areas — often in combination.

01
Organizational Strategy & Leadership Advisory

Strategic planning, executive coaching, and organizational design for mission-aligned nonprofits and civic institutions. Particularly suited to transitions, launches, and moments of inflection.

02
Coalition Building & Field Navigation

Drawing on a national network across the democracy, civic health, and governance space to help organizations build partnerships, navigate the funding landscape, and activate the right relationships.

03
Policy Research & Data Analysis

Rigorous qualitative and quantitative research to ground strategy in evidence. Translating complex data and policy landscapes into clear, actionable narratives for leadership and external audiences.

04
Communications & Narrative Development

Crafting the institutional story: board presentations, funder communications, public narratives, and the through-lines that connect mission to momentum.

05
Interim & Fractional Leadership

Stepping in at the executive level during transitions or high-stakes moments — bringing full organizational leadership capacity without a permanent commitment.

06
Sector Landscape & Due Diligence

Deep-dive research into the democracy and civic health field for funders, investors, or organizations seeking to understand where to place their resources and relationships.

How It Works

The Eleven Insight approach.

Every engagement starts with listening. The frameworks come second.

Network-native, not network-adjacent

A decade embedded in the national pro-democracy and civic field means the right introductions, context, and read on the landscape come built in — not billed separately as research time.

Operational rigor at the strategic level

Many strategists can tell you what to do. Fewer understand how organizations actually function — the budget realities, the staff dynamics, the board relationships that make or break execution. Both lenses are always in the room.

Data fluency without data for its own sake

Quantitative skills are most useful when they serve clarity, not complexity. The goal is always a decision or a narrative — not a methodology.

Honest counsel, not comfortable answers

The highest-value advisory relationships are the ones where hard things get said early, not late. Eleven Insight operates as a thought partner, not a vendor.
